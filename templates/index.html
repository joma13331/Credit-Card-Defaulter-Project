Credit Card Default Prediction

This Web Application implements a Machine Learning Solution to determine whether a customer will default on next month's payment based of his/her previous activities.
Any bank's future investments and financial activities are dependent on the amount of return from its previous investments and assets. Hence, predicting next month's defaulters will help the bank make a better estimate on how much financial activity it can carry out.
In order to determine this the following details are required:

  1. ID: ID of each client
  2. LIMIT_BAL: Amount of given credit
  3. SEX: Gender (1=male, 2=female)
  4. EDUCATION: (1=graduate school, 2=university, 3=high school, 4=others, 5=unknown, 6=unknown)
  5. MARRIAGE: Marital status (1=married, 2=single, 3=others)
  6. PAY_1: Repayment status for previous month (-2=no activity, -1=pay duly, 1=payment delay for one month, 2=payment delay for two months, … 8=payment delay for eight months, 9=payment delay for nine months and above)
  7. PAY_2: Repayment status for 2nd month previous to present month (-2=no activity, -1=pay duly, 1=payment delay for one month, 2=payment delay for two months, … 8=payment delay for eight months, 9=payment delay for nine months and above)
  8. PAY_3: Repayment status for 3rd month previous to present month (-2=no activity, -1=pay duly, 1=payment delay for one month, 2=payment delay for two months, … 8=payment delay for eight months, 9=payment delay for nine months and above)
  9. PAY_4: Repayment status for 4th month previous to present month (-2=no activity, -1=pay duly, 1=payment delay for one month, 2=payment delay for two months, … 8=payment delay for eight months, 9=payment delay for nine months and above)
  10. PAY_5: Repayment status for 5th month previous to present month (-2=no activity, -1=pay duly, 1=payment delay for one month, 2=payment delay for two months, … 8=payment delay for eight months, 9=payment delay for nine months and above)
  11. PAY_6: Repayment status for 6th month previous to present month (-2=no activity, -1=pay duly, 1=payment delay for one month, 2=payment delay for two months, … 8=payment delay for eight months, 9=payment delay for nine months and above)
  12. BILL_AMT1: Amount of bill statement for previous month
  13. BILL_AMT2: Amount of bill statement for 2nd month previous to present month
  14. BILL_AMT3: Amount of bill statement for 3rd month previous to present month
  15. BILL_AMT4: Amount of bill statement for 4th month previous to present month
  16. BILL_AMT5: Amount of bill statement for 5th month previous to present month
  17. BILL_AMT6: Amount of bill statement for 6th month previous to present month
  18. PAY_AMT1: Amount of payment for previous month
  19. PAY_AMT2: Amount of payment for 2nd month previous to present month
  20. PAY_AMT3: Amount of payment for 3rd month previous to present month
  21. PAY_AMT4: Amount of payment for 4th month previous to present month
  22. PAY_AMT5: Amount of payment for 5th month previous to present month
  23. PAY_AMT6: Amount of payment for 6th month previous to present month

Predict

To get a prediction on Concrete Compressive Strength upload a .csv (Comma Separated Value) file in the following format

  • Filename in format "Credit_Card_{DDMMYYYY}_{HHMMSS}data.csv"
    Where "DDMMYYYY" represents the date and "HHMMSS" represents the time.
  • Column Names in the first row in following format:
    1. First Column Heading: "ID"
    2. Second Column Heading: "LIMIT_BAL"
    3. Third Column Heading: "SEX"
    4. Fourth Column Heading: "EDUCATION"
    5. Fifth Column Heading: "MARRIAGE"
    6. Sixth Column Heading: "PAY_1"
    7. Seventh Column Heading: "PAY_2"
    8. Eighth Column Heading: "PAY_3"
    9. Ninth Column Heading: "PAY_4"
    10. Tenth Column Heading: "PAY_5"
    11. Eleventh Column Heading: "PAY_6"
    12. Twelfth Column Heading: "BILL_AMT1"
    13. Thirteenth Column Heading: "BILL_AMT2"
    14. Fourteenth Column Heading: "BILL_AMT3"
    15. Fifteenth Column Heading: "BILL_AMT4"
    16. Sixteenth Column Heading: "BILL_AMT5"
    17. Seventeenth Column Heading: "BILL_AMT6"
    18. Eighteenth Column Heading: "PAY_AMT1"
    19. Nineteenth Column Heading: "PAY_AMT2"
    20. Twentieth Column Heading: "PAY_AMT3"
    21. Twenty First Column Heading: "PAY_AMT4"
    22. Twenty Second Column Heading: "PAY_AMT5"
    23. Twenty Third Column Heading: "PAY_AMT6"
  • The data to be predicted on should start from 2nd row
